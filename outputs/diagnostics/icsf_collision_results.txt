## Key reading
Even in the exact cell where full IGSTGNN beat FDN (+1.135 affected MAE on CC
collision_unkinj windows), ICSF-on-GWN moves only -0.02 to -0.12 (within seed
noise). Conclusion: IGSTGNN's residual advantage on CC collision windows is NOT
attributable to its incident-fusion module; it must come from the backbone /
training dynamics. The label-signal lead for an architecture innovation is
therefore much weaker than the type-breakdown alone suggested.
