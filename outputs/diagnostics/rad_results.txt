CONCLUSION: RAD-GMixer is a clean NEGATIVE. It is the WEAKEST model tried -- worse than even the
node-local RFN-v0 TCN (+0.28), far from GWN-v0c (+1.03). The multi-scale direct-linear temporal
mixer (DLinear-style pooled+linear) is weaker than a causal TCN; it loses temporal detail and
lands near pure-baseline level (anchor + a residual head too weak to help). affected is terrible
(19.0 vs v0c 17.2). The constrained local road graph helps only -0.083, nowhere near GWN's
adaptive graph (~0.5). Third confirmation that GWN's dilated-TCN + adaptive-graph is genuinely
strong and NOT replaceable by a lightweight independent backbone.

IMPLICATION: the v0c regime-adaptive-decomposition mechanism is real but BACKBONE-DEPENDENT -- it
needs a strong backbone (GWN) to shine; lightweight independent backbones (RFN TCN, RAD mixer)
cannot match GWN. The "independent new main model" bet (RFN, RAD) has failed twice. Consolidate
around v0c (GWN + adaptive de-seasonalization) as the paper's contribution: a lightweight
label-free mechanism that improves a strong backbone, framed honestly as backbone-dependent.
