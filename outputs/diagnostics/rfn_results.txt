  Also: qov is validated and scales (-0.146 at matched params).

IMPLICATION: RFN's next rung must be the GRAPH (v2 directed lagged road transition), not more
capacity. But the climb is steep and honest: even a graph that fully matches GWN only reaches
~11.8, then RFN needs de-season/regime/qov stacked to reach v0c (11.452) / STAEformer (11.391).
Decision point: build v2 road graph (pivotal test: does the directed-lagged road graph close the
~0.5 gap to GWN?), or graft the validated qov idea onto the GWN backbone instead.
