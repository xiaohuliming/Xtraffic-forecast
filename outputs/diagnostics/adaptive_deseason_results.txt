interpretation (HONEST)
1. v0c is a clean, reproducible, equiparam, multi-seed WIN over v0b. First architecture-side
   gain in the project after 6 straight enhancement failures. It is label-free.
2. alpha DOES differentiate by anomaly: lower on affected (0.81) than unaffected (0.89), as
   designed. So the mechanism is not a degenerate constant gate (unlike the D3/v1g traps).
3. BUT the optimizer overrode the relu pin: r0 collapsed from init 1.5 to ~0.03 and tau grew
   from 1.0 to ~4.0. So alpha = exp(-r/4) applies to ALL nodes, not just anomalous ones; even
   normal nodes get ~11% persistence mixed into the anchor (alpha~0.89). The learned solution
   is a GLOBAL persistence-blend that leans harder on persistence for anomalous nodes, not the
   restricted "leave normal nodes at alpha=1" design. It generalized to a better solution.
4. Consequence / caveat: an unknown share of the -0.229 may come from the global persistence
   blend (a known residual/persistence-hybrid idea) rather than the anomaly-specific weighting.
   The affected-vs-unaffected alpha gap (0.81 vs 0.89) is modest. Next ablation should
   disentangle: a CONSTANT-alpha variant (learned scalar blend of baseline+persistence, no
   r-dependence). If constant-alpha captures most of -0.229, the adaptive part is minor; if
   adaptive clearly beats constant, the anomaly weighting is the real lever.
