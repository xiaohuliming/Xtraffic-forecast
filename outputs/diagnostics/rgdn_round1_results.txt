=== interpretation (single seed 42, Alameda, params matched to ~316k) ===

1. De-seasonalization is the only real gain. v0b beats v0a by 0.100 on all MAE at
   identical params 315,648. The gain comes from a single GWN predicting only the
   deviation from the cache climatology baseline. No dual structure is involved.

2. The RGDN dual residual-guided mechanism does not help. v1 is 0.174 worse than v0b
   on all MAE, while v1 even has slightly fewer params 308,660. The asymmetric dual
   plus injection underperforms the simple de-seasonalized single GWN.

3. The neighbor-residual injection actively hurts. v1 with injection is 0.105 worse
   than v2 without it. The core novel element of the design degrades accuracy.

4. The dual split alone is roughly neutral. v2 dual no-injection vs v0b single is
   +0.068 on all, inside the seed noise band, so splitting into two branches buys
   nothing over one de-seasonalized GWN.

5. Who de-seasonalization helps. v0b improves unaffected nodes by 0.119 but worsens
   affected nodes by 0.200 vs v0a. The climatology baseline fits normal regimes;
   incident-affected nodes deviate from it, so adding the baseline back helps normal
   nodes and slightly hurts affected ones.

caveats. Single seed 42 only, suggestive not definitive. The v1-worse direction is
about 2x the noise band so fairly clear, but seeds 1 and 2 plus significance_tests.py
would confirm. The first launch of v1 was killed mid-training by an environmental
process kill at ep17; the rerun completed all of v1 and v2 cleanly (DONE 04:23).

bottom line. The architectural hypothesis is not supported on Alameda. De-seasonalization
is a small clean win achievable with a single GWN, which matches the project pattern that
added structure does not beat simple label-free baselines. Next step options: confirm
v0b vs v1 with seeds 1 and 2 before closing, or accept the negative and keep
de-seasonalization as a minor element while dropping the RGDN mechanism.

=== gated injection follow-up (v1g, learnable scalar gate init 1.0) ===

v1g all=11.922 affected=18.097 unaffected=11.537 params=308,661. The learnable injection
gate, free to drop to 0 and turn injection off, settled at 0.898, and v1g landed at 11.922,
worse than v2 11.779 and no better than v1 11.884.

So the injection is not an ignorable add-on the model would switch off if it could; the
model commits to it. The diagnostics explain why (scripts/diagnose_rgdn_injection.py): the
injection adjacency degenerates to near-uniform, entropy ratio 0.987, so the injection is a
crude global-average residual shortcut; the model becomes dependent on it, zeroing it at
inference collapses v1; and a learnable gate does not escape the trap, gate stays at 0.90.
The neighbor-residual injection is an optimization trap offering an inferior spatial path
versus the residual branch, and it cannot be salvaged by gating.

final conclusion. Drop the residual-guided injection. The clean architecture is the
de-seasonalized single GWN v0b or the dual no-injection v2, which tie. The only real gain in
this whole family is de-seasonalization, predicting the deviation from the cache climatology
baseline, about 0.10 MAE on all, and it needs no extra structure. Consistent with the
project pattern that added structure does not beat simple label-free baselines. Single seed
42; multi-seed would tighten the bands but the direction is clear and the mechanism is
explained.
